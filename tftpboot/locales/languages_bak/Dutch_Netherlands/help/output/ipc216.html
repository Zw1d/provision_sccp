Understanding Feature Functionality and Availability

The operation of your Cisco IP Communicator and the features available to you may vary, depending on the call processing agent used by your company and also on how your company's phone support team has configured your phone system. Therefore, some of the features included in this User Guide might not be available to you or might work differently on your phone system. Contact your support desk or system administrator if you have questions about feature operation or availability.
