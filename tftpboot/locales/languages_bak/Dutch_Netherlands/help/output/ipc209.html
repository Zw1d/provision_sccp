Answer a call

  • Click the anywhere on the pop-up box (except on the mute icon)

Mute the ringer

  • Click mute icon on the pop-up box

Tip     If you mute the ringer on the Incoming Call Notification pop-up box, you must remember to disable mute to avoid muting the ringer for all subsequent incoming calls.
