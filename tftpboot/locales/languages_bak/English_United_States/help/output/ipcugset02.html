Where to Access Settings

Here is some useful information to keep in mind about Cisco IP Communicator settings:

  • Most settings are accessible by choosing Preferences from the menu. You access the menu from the menu icon in the window control button bar, by right-clicking anywhere on the interface, or by pressing Shift + F10.
  • Ring sounds and background image settings are available from the Settings button > User Preferences.
  • Most settings are accessible on your IP Communicator, but a few are accessed online from your User Options web pages.

Note     If the Settings button is not responsive, your system administrator might have disabled this button on your phone. Ask your system administrator for more information.
