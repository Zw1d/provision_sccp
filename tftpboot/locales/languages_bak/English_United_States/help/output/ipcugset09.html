Optimize for Low Bandwidth

If you are using Cisco IP Communicator over a remote connection (for example, on a VPN connection from home or a hotel), voice quality might suffer from insufficient bandwidth. When you are using Cisco IP Communicator over a remote connection, you can prevent robotic-sounding audio and other problems by enabling Optimize for Low Bandwidth.
